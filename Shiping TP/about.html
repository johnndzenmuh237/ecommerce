Our Story

Built on Trust Since 1998

SwiftCargo was founded in Los Angeles by a small team of logistics veterans who believed freight forwarding could be done better — with greater transparency, smarter technology, and genuine care for clients.

What started as a regional ocean freight broker has grown into a full-service global logistics provider with offices in 45 countries, 1,200+ employees, and the trust of over 4,500 businesses ranging from SMEs to Fortune 500 corporations.

Our mission has never changed: deliver your cargo safely, on time, and at a fair price — while providing the visibility and support that lets you focus on your business.
