Service Options

Choose Your Container Solution

📦

FCL — Full Container Load

Your cargo occupies the entire container. Best for shipments over 12–15 CBM or when cargo security, handling control, or faster transit is a priority.

  • ✓  20ft, 40ft, 40ft HC, 45ft options
  • ✓  Reefer containers available
  • ✓  Open-top & flat rack for OOG cargo
  • ✓  Faster transit vs LCL
Best for: 12+ CBM shipments
📮

LCL — Less than Container Load

Share container space with other shippers. Pay only for the space your cargo occupies. Ideal for smaller shipments that don't require a full container.

  • ✓  Charged per CBM or weight ton
  • ✓  Weekly consolidations
  • ✓  Cost-effective for small volumes
  • ✓  No minimum cargo size
Best for: 1–12 CBM shipments
❄️

Reefer Containers

Temperature-controlled FCL and LCL for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive cargo across all major trade lanes.

  • ✓  -30°C to +30°C range
  • ✓  Controlled atmosphere options
  • ✓  24/7 temperature monitoring
  • ✓  GDP-compliant cold chain
Best for: Perishables & Pharma
